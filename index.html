01 — ABOUT US

We exist to close the gap between what you imagine
and what gets built.

Ritual & Stone Studio is a remote-first luxury bathroom design consultancy based in Australia. We help homeowners and renovators define their design vision before the build begins — so every tile, every fitting, every finish is chosen with intention.

Our services are delivered entirely through video consultations, curated mood boards, and detailed specification guides that serve as your blueprint for every conversation with a builder or supplier.

Whether you are redesigning a single ensuite or building a sanctuary from the ground up, we bring the same precision and expertise to every project.

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Ced Fabillar-Roznicki

Founder & Principal Consultant

With years of experience as a Porcelanosa consultant, Ced brings deep knowledge of luxury materials, European design standards, and the precise decision-making required to achieve a truly exceptional bathroom.

She founded Ritual & Stone Studio to bring that same level of expert guidance directly to Australian homeowners — entirely remotely, without compromise on quality or detail.

Ced believes the most important decisions in a renovation happen long before the first tile is laid. Her role is to ensure yours are made with clarity, confidence, and intention.
